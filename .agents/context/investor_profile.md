# Investor Profile

This file captures the investor's personal context. Reference it in every recommendation, advisor council response, and rebalancing suggestion.

**Last updated:** April 2026

## Identity

- Investing in Indian equity markets (BSE/NSE)
- Sharia-compliant investing is a hard constraint — non-negotiable filter applied before any other analysis
- Uses this repo as the primary tool for investment decisions

## Goals and Horizon

- **Primary goal:** Beat inflation + steady compounding
- **Not:** Maximum CAGR at all costs, nor capital preservation
- **Investment horizon:** 7–10 years
- **Approach:** Long-term buy-and-hold of quality businesses; not a trader

## Risk Tolerance

- **Drawdown behaviour:** Rational — does not panic-sell; reviews with this tool during drawdowns; buys cautiously if conviction and cash are available
- **Emotional risk:** Low to moderate — uses structured framework (advisor council) before acting; aware of FOMO and panic biases
- **Comfortable with:** Mid and small cap volatility if holding period is sufficient

## Deployment Method

- Monthly SIP (fixed amount, rupee-cost averaging)
- Opportunistic lump sums on quality dips (momentum score ≤ 40 + Piotroski ≥ 5)
- No leverage, no derivatives

## What This Means for Recommendations

- Always check sector weight before suggesting a new stock — fill gaps first
- Prefer quality (Piotroski ≥ 5, red_flags ≤ 2) over cheapness alone
- Dip-buying is acceptable and encouraged when fundamentals are intact
- Do not recommend stocks that are non-Sharia compliant under any framing
- When uncertain, prefer "watch and wait" over "act now"
